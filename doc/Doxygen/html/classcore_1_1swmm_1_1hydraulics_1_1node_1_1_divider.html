Detailed Description

Flow Dividers are drainage system nodes that divert inflows to a specific conduit in a prescribed manner.

A flow divider can have no more than two conduit links on its discharge side. Flow dividers are only active under Kinematic Wave routing and are treated as simple junctions under Dynamic Wave routing.
